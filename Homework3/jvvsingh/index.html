Data Science Salaries Dashboard

Instructions:
  • Click: A bar in the bar chart to select a job title and highlight it across all charts. The line chart will show only that job title's salary trend.
  • Drag: In the scatter plot to select a group of data points (brushing). Selected points and corresponding bars/lines are highlighted.
  • Click: A year above or a dot in the line chart to update all charts to that year.
  • Click: A colored area in the stream graph or a line in the parallel coordinates to select a job title and update all charts.
  • Hover: Over any chart element for details. Charts animate on update.

Bar Chart: Average Salary by Job Title (by Year)

Bar color: Salary in USD. X: Job Title, Y: Average Salary, Animated by Year.

Scatter Plot: Salary (USD) vs. Experience Level (by Year)

X: Experience Level, Y: Salary (USD), Animated by Year. Drag to brush.

Parallel Coordinates: Salary, Remote Ratio, Experience, Company Location

Each line: one record. Axes: Salary, Remote Ratio, Experience Level, Company Location.

Line & Area Chart: Average Salary by Year

X: Year, Y: Average Salary. Area and line both show average salary trend or selected job title. Dot color = year. Click a dot to select a year.

Stream Graph: Job Titles Over Time

Each color: a job title. X: Year, Y: Count. Click an area to select a job title.
